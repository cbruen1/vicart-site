Commissions

Portraits

One of the most personal and long-lasting presents are probably professional drawings and paintings. The great thing about them is that you can make each one of them unique for every occasion and person. They are a great gift for any birthday, wedding, graduation or just a way to tell someone you care about them. With a nice frame and a couple of warm words it could became the perfect present.

The most important thing that I need for a commissioned work is a photo. Size is crucial, the bigger the better. If you would like a commissioned artwork contact me via email so we could discuss every aspect (size, quality, number of people, backround, etc.).

Other

You can order any other type of painting/drawing such as landscapes, seascapes, nature, fantasy etc. What the eye can see, the hand can draw.
