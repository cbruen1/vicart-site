My work

My name is Victor, and what I do is drawing and painting. Usually I work with graphite pencil, colored pencils, oil paints and charcoal. What I like to do most is realism.

Quick Biography and Artist Statement

Born in Switzerland Victor was raised and schooled in Croatia. A relative newcomer to the art community, Victor has only been drawing seriously for about 4 years. He has always been keen on the pencil although he has dabbled in other media such as oils and watercolors. His favourite subjects so far have been portraits of everyday people and lately he has sought to create original material using his own photography.
