Me

Quick Biography and Artist Statement

My name is Victor S Bjelakovic and I was born in 1991 in the sweet little town of Lugano, Switzerland. After several years there, my life continued in a few different cities in the Balkans. Since a young age I was fascinated with creativity and art in any form. I have finished trade school but, unfortunately, I was never able to go to art school. That dream still lives on.

At the moment I live in Opatija, Croatia and, work aside, I'm trying to dedicate as much time as possible to painting and drawing. In my free time I also play in a band. Some of my idols in the art world are Victor Powell, Guy Denning, Banksy etc. I consider my craft is at it's best drawing with graphite pencils, but trying to expand that to charcoal and oil. With art you learn every day.

My dream at the moment is to have a solo or group exibithion and establish my name in the art scene. Feel free to contact me for commissions or any other reason ;)
